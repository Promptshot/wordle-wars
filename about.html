What is Wordle Wars?

Wordle Wars is a competitive word-guessing game where you can challenge other players for real money. Create a game with your wager, wait for an opponent to join, and race to guess the secret word first.

Your funds are held securely on the Solana blockchain until the game completes. Winner takes the pot.

How to Play

  • Connect your Phantom wallet
  • Create a game with your chosen wager (minimum 0.022 SOL)
  • Wait for an opponent to join
  • Guess the 5-letter word before your opponent (6 tries each)
  • Winner gets the pot

Fees & Payouts

Win a game You get 98% of pot
Both players lose House wins the pot
Cancel before opponent joins 100% refund
Platform fee 2% on wins

All transactions happen on-chain. Your funds are always secure and verifiable.
